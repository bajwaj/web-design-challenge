Wind Speed

Wind speed is widely distributed and has little correlation with latitude. However, as you get further away from 0 degrees latitude and move further towards the poles the wind speed seems to increase. You can see this on the graph on the ends where there are some points that have wind speeds as higher then 30 mph.
